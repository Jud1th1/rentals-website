FAQs

What equipment do I need for my specific event?

"The equipment you need for your specific event will depend on a variety of factors such as the size of the venue, the number of guests, and the type of event you are hosting. We recommend that you provide us with as much information as possible about your event when you inquire about renting equipment. Our experienced team can provide you with expert advice on the equipment you'll need based on your specific requirements. We can work with you to create a customized equipment rental package that fits your event's unique needs and budget. Contact us today to discuss your event and we'll be happy to provide you with equipment recommendations and rental options."

Can I rent equipment for a single day or do I need to rent it for a longer period of time?

"We offer equipment rentals for a variety of durations to accommodate your needs. You can rent our equipment for a single day, a weekend, a week, or even longer periods of time. Our rental periods are flexible and can be customized to fit your specific event or project needs. We also offer discounted rates for longer rental periods. Please contact us to discuss your specific rental needs and we'll be happy to help you choose the right rental period for your event or project."

How do I set up and operate the equipment I am renting?

"We understand that setting up and operating audio equipment can be intimidating if you're not familiar with it. That's why we are happy to provide instructions and guidance for setting up and operating the equipment you are renting. When you rent equipment from us, we will provide you with a detailed instruction manual or guide that will walk you through the process of setting up and operating the equipment. Additionally, we are available to answer any questions you may have before, during, or after your rental period. If you would like hands-on assistance with setup and operation, we also offer delivery and setup services for an additional fee. Our experienced technicians will ensure that the equipment is set up correctly and provide a brief tutorial on how to use it. Please let us know if you have any questions or concerns about setting up and operating the equipment you are renting."

Is delivery and setup included in the rental price?

"We offer delivery and setup services for an additional fee. The cost of delivery and setup may vary depending on the equipment and the location of the rental. If you would like to add delivery and setup to your rental, please let us know when you make your reservation and we will provide you with a quote. We understand that some customers prefer to handle their own setup, so we offer the option to pick up the equipment from our location as well. If you have any questions about delivery and setup, please don't hesitate to contact us."

Is there a deposit required to rent equipment?

"We require a deposit to reserve your rental equipment. The deposit amount may vary depending on the equipment and the length of the rental period. The deposit will be refunded to you when the equipment is returned in good condition at the end of the rental period. Please note that any damage to the equipment may result in a deduction from the deposit refund. We take care to inspect all equipment before and after each rental to ensure that any damage is documented and addressed. If you have any questions about the deposit requirement, please don't hesitate to contact us."

What is the cancellation policy for equipment rentals?

"Our cancellation policy allows for full refunds for cancellations made 48 hours or more before the rental start time. If you need to cancel your rental within 48 hours of the start time, you may be subject to a cancellation fee. If you fail to show up for your rental, you will not be eligible for a refund. We understand that unforeseen circumstances can arise, so please don't hesitate to contact us if you need to cancel or reschedule your rental. We will do our best to work with you to find a solution that works for everyone."
